Spirituality (2018)

"This piece is based on my interest in yoga and meditation. The anatomical heart represents her passion for life bursting out as she sits and meditates surrounded by nature, the sun and the moon. She finds herself connected to her surroundings. You can see she is connected to the roots of the tree and both are growing strong even among the stormy gray clouds that appear to be in the background." ~ Karina

Mujer Divina (2019)

"This painting is about resilience. It is about choosing to stay strong among difficult times. The corazon de nopal represents life, its good and difficult at times. You can see she is holding the heart as blood drips on her hands. She may be in pain but she is still standing strong and as she remains strong and firme something amazing happens inside of her. You can see that there is growth happening inside of her as a beautiful vine of the passion fruit flower reaches out of her in search of light and life. This represent her passion for life and growth regardless of the difficulties and challenges of life she is still standing strong." ~ Karina

"Mujer Divina"
