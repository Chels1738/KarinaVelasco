Mariposas y Chile Rojos (2024)

A young child in the fields picking chile rojos. The chile rojos are a symbol of the struggle and resilience of the Mexican people. The mariposas represent hope and the beauty of life. The child wears a Hello Kitty bag to symbolize the innocence and the beauty of childhood but the cruelty of the situation is not lost on the viewer. Yet the painting is not dark or depressing, it is a celebration of life and the resilience of the human spirit.

Hope (2021)

“Been thinking a lot about life lately, mainly about human existence and about how even on my darkest night I have been blessed with Hope. Life has been pretty crazy these days. I thought a lot about my Abuelita who recently passed away while I created this piece. She was a firm believer in la Virgen De Guadalupe and would always pray for her loved ones to be protected con el manto de la Virgencita, somehow now remembering her prayers makes me feel safe ,...so I tried to wrapped this baby con en el manto tambien. My goal for this piece was mainly to illustrate Hope and passion for life” ~ Karina

Yo Soy Vida (2022)

A young woman with flowers growing out of her body represents the power each woman has to create life. The flowers represent the beauty of life and the power of creation. The young woman is strong and confident, radiating life and vitality. She is vida.
